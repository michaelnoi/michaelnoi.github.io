Michael Neumayr

PhD in Computer Science @ UofT

I am a PhD student in Computer Science at the University of Toronto, working on 3D computer vision and generative models under the supervision of David Lindell. I hold an M.Sc. in Computer Science from TU Munich and a B.Sc. in Computer Science and B.A. in Philosophy from the University of Munich.

My M.Sc. thesis was at the University of Toronto with Sherwin Bahmani and David Lindell. I worked as a research assistant at the Ommerlab with Stephan Baumann. Master’s courses included a project on generalizable novel view synthesis with David Rozenberszki and research on diffusion models in medical imaging with Cosmin Bercea and Julia Schnabel.
